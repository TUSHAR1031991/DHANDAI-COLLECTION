About Us

Founded in 2010 in nashik, Dhandai Collection began as a humble passion for Indian textiles and has grown into a respected global brand known for elegance, craftsmanship, and authenticity.

At Dhandai Collection, we specialize in premium sarees and traditional textile materials, blending Indian heritage with modern fashion sensibilities.

Over the years, we’ve built strong relationships with artisans and weavers across India, ensuring that each piece we offer carries a story — of culture, color, and care. From silk sarees to cotton blends, our collection is curated to meet the diverse tastes of our global customers.

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains the same: delivering beauty, quality, and cultural richness with every fabric we share.
